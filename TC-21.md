# Acceptance Test Suite — UC-21 Make Final Paper Decision

## Overview
**Use Case:** UC-21 Make Final Paper Decision  
**Primary Actor:** Editor  
**Objective:** Verify that an editor can make and record a final accept/reject decision **only when exactly three completed reviews exist**, that the decision is stored correctly, the author is notified, and all failure and exception paths are handled safely.

---

## Assumptions / Test Data
- Supported browsers: Chrome, Firefox (latest stable).
- A paper must have **exactly three completed review forms** before a decision can be made.
- Only editors may record final decisions.
- Decision options are limited to **Accept** or **Reject**.
- Notifications may be asynchronous (e.g., email or in-system alert).

## UC-21-AT-01 — Editor can access decision option for eligible paper (Main Success Path)
**Priority:** P0  
**Preconditions:** Editor authenticated; P-01 has exactly 3 completed reviews.  
**Steps:**
1. Log in as editor (E-01).
2. Navigate to the list of submitted papers.
3. Select P-01.
**Expected Results:**
- CMS displays completed review forms.
- CMS displays decision options (Accept / Reject).
- Decision action is enabled.

---

## UC-21-AT-02 — Accept decision is recorded successfully
**Priority:** P0  
**Test Data:** E-01 + P-01  
**Preconditions:** P-01 has exactly 3 completed reviews.  
**Steps:**
1. Open P-01 as editor.
2. Select **Accept**.
3. Confirm the decision.
**Expected Results:**
- CMS validates that all required reviews are complete.
- CMS records the decision as “Accepted” in the database.
- CMS confirms successful decision recording to editor.

---

## UC-21-AT-03 — Reject decision is recorded successfully
**Priority:** P0  
**Test Data:** E-01 + P-01  
**Steps:**
1. Open P-01 as editor.
2. Select **Reject**.
3. Confirm the decision.
**Expected Results:**
- CMS records the decision as “Rejected”.

## UC-21-AT-04 — Author receives notification of decision
**Priority:** P1  
**Test Data:** A-01 + P-01  
**Preconditions:** A decision has been recorded for P-01.  
**Steps:**
1. Wait for notification delivery.
2. Log in as author (A-01).
3. Navigate to submitted papers.
**Expected Results:**
- Author receives a notification (email or in-system).
- Author can view the final decision in CMS.

---

## UC-21-AT-05 — Decision blocked when fewer than three reviews exist (Extension 7a)
**Priority:** P0  
**Test Data:** E-01 + P-02  
**Preconditions:** P-02 has fewer than 3 completed reviews.  
**Steps:**
1. Log in as editor.
2. Select P-02.
3. Attempt to make a final decision.
**Expected Results:**
- CMS blocks the decision process.
- CMS informs editor that additional reviews are required.
- No decision is recorded.

---

## UC-21-AT-06 — Editor cancels decision before confirmation (Extension 6a)
**Priority:** P1  
**Test Data:** E-01 + P-01  
**Steps:**
1. Open decision interface for P-01.
2. Select a decision.
3. Cancel before confirming.
**Expected Results:**
- CMS abandons decision process.
- No decision is stored.
- Editor is returned to paper list or details view.

---

## UC-21-AT-07 — Database unavailable prevents saving decision (Extension 8a)
**Priority:** P0  
**Test Data:** E-01 + P-01  
**Preconditions:** Simulate CMS database outage.  
**Steps:**
1. Attempt to record a decision for P-01.
**Expected Results:**
- CMS displays error indicating decision could not be saved.
- Decision is not recorded.
- Author is not notified.

---

## UC-21-AT-08 — Notification failure does not block decision visibility (Extension 9a)
**Priority:** P1  
**Preconditions:** Simulate notification service failure.  
**Steps:**
1. Editor records a valid decision.
2. Log in as author.
**Expected Results:**
- CMS logs notification failure.
- Author can still view decision upon logging into CMS.
- No data loss occurs.

---

## UC-21-AT-09 — Editor must be authenticated to make decisions
**Priority:** P0  
**Preconditions:** Editor session expired or logged out.  
**Steps:**
1. Attempt to access decision page directly via URL.
**Expected Results:**
- CMS prompts editor to log in.
- Decision options are not accessible until authenticated.

---

## UC-21-AT-10 — Web server unavailable behavior (Extension 1a)
**Priority:** P0  
**Preconditions:** Simulate CMS web server outage.  
**Steps:**
1. Attempt to access or submit a final decision.
**Expected Results:**
- CMS displays system error page.
- No decision is recorded.

---

## UC-21-AT-11 — Security: non-editor cannot make final decisions
**Priority:** P0  
**Preconditions:** Non-editor account exists (author or referee).  
**Steps:**
1. Log in as non-editor user.
2. Attempt to access decision interface.
**Expected Results:**
- CMS denies access (403 or safe redirect).
- Decision controls are not visible or usable.
