# Acceptance Test Suite — UC-12 Receive Presentation Schedule

## Overview
**Use Case:** UC-12 Receive Presentation Schedule  
**Primary Actor:** Author  
**Objective:** Verify that once the conference schedule is finalized and published, authors of accepted papers can view their presentation time and location, and that notification, authentication, and failure scenarios are handled correctly.

---

## Assumptions / Test Data
- Supported browsers: Chrome, Firefox (latest stable).
- Authors must be authenticated to view schedule details in the CMS.
- Only **accepted** papers receive presentation schedules.
- Notification is sent via email (or equivalent), but the schedule must always be viewable in the CMS even if notification fails.

## UC-12-AT-01 — Schedule cannot be published before finalization (Extension 1a)
**Priority:** P0  
**Test Data:** AD-01 or E-01  
**Preconditions:** Conference schedule is not finalized.  
**Steps:**
1. Log in as administrator or editor.
2. Attempt to publish or notify authors of the schedule.
**Expected Results:**
- CMS blocks publication/notification.
- CMS indicates that the schedule is still pending.
- No schedule notification is sent to authors.

---

## UC-12-AT-02 — Finalized schedule is stored and triggers notification (Main Success Path)
**Priority:** P0  
**Test Data:** AD-01 + S-01  
**Preconditions:** Schedule has been generated and marked as finalized.  
**Steps:**
1. Log in as administrator (AD-01).
2. Publish the finalized schedule.
**Expected Results:**
- CMS stores the finalized schedule in the database.
- CMS triggers notifications to authors of accepted papers.
- Administrator receives confirmation that the schedule was published.

---

## UC-12-AT-03 — Author can view presentation schedule after login (Main Success Path)
**Priority:** P0  
**Test Data:** A-01 + P-01 + S-01  
**Preconditions:** Schedule for P-01 exists in DB and is published.  
**Steps:**
1. Log in as author (A-01).
2. Navigate to “My Submissions”.
3. Select accepted paper P-01.
**Expected Results:**
- CMS displays presentation time and location from S-01.
- Information is clearly labeled and readable.
- No schedule is shown for rejected papers.

---

## UC-12-AT-04 — Notification failure does not block schedule viewing (Extension 3a)
**Priority:** P1  
**Test Data:** AD-01 + A-01 + P-01  
**Preconditions:** Simulate notification service failure during publication.  
**Steps:**
1. Publish finalized schedule while notification service is unavailable.
2. Log in as author (A-01).
3. Navigate to accepted paper P-01.
**Expected Results:**
- CMS logs the notification failure.
- Schedule is still stored in DB.
- Author can view schedule after login.

---

## UC-12-AT-05 — Unauthenticated author is prompted to log in (Extension 4a)
**Priority:** P1  
**Test Data:** P-01 + S-01  
**Preconditions:** User is logged out.  
**Steps:**
1. Attempt to access schedule details directly (if URL known) or access “My Submissions”.
**Expected Results:**
- CMS redirects user to login or prompts authentication.
- Schedule details are not shown to unauthenticated users.

---

## UC-12-AT-06 — Schedule information unavailable shows error (Extension 6a)
**Priority:** P0  
**Test Data:** A-01 + P-01  
**Preconditions:** Schedule exists but retrieval fails (simulate DB read error or missing record).  
**Steps:**
1. Log in as author (A-01).
2. Navigate to accepted paper P-01 schedule view.
**Expected Results:**
- CMS displays a user-friendly error indicating schedule cannot be retrieved.
- No sensitive technical details are exposed.

---

## UC-12-AT-07 — Database unavailable prevents schedule publication (Extension 2a)
**Priority:** P0  
**Test Data:** AD-01  
**Preconditions:** Simulate DB outage during schedule publication.  
**Steps:**
1. Log in as administrator.
2. Attempt to publish finalized schedule while DB is unavailable.
**Expected Results:**
- CMS displays an error indicating publication failed.
- Schedule is not marked as published.
- Author notifications are delayed until successful storage.

---

## UC-12-AT-08 — Only accepted papers receive schedule information
**Priority:** P1  
**Test Data:** A-01 + P-02  
**Preconditions:** Schedule is published for accepted papers.  
**Steps:**
1. Log in as author.
2. Navigate to rejected paper P-02.
**Expected Results:**
- CMS does not display presentation schedule for rejected papers.
- CMS may display a message indicating the paper was not accepted.

---

## UC-12-AT-09 — Web server unavailable behavior
**Priority:** P0  
**Preconditions:** Simulate CMS web server down/unreachable.  
**Steps:**
1. Attempt to load login, submissions page, or schedule view.
**Expected Results:**
- User receives unavailability response (browser error or CMS error page).
- If CMS error page is shown, it indicates temporary service unavailability.

---

## UC-12-AT-10 — Cross-browser schedule viewing is consistent
**Priority:** P2  
**Test Data:** A-01 + P-01 + S-01  
**Preconditions:** None.  
**Steps:**
1. View presentation schedule in Chrome.
2. View presentation schedule in Firefox.
**Expected Results:**
- Schedule time and location display consistently across browsers.
