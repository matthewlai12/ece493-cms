# Acceptance Test Suite — UC-25 Publish Final Conference Schedule

## Overview
**Use Case:** UC-25 Publish Final Conference Schedule  
**Primary Actor:** Administrator  
**Objective:** Verify that a finalized conference schedule can be published successfully, becomes publicly accessible, and that relevant stakeholders are notified. Ensure all failure and cancellation paths are handled safely.

---

## Assumptions / Test Data
- Supported browsers: Chrome, Firefox (latest stable).
- Only administrators can publish schedules.
- A finalized and validated conference schedule exists.
- Notification service supports email delivery to authors and attendees.

## UC-25-AT-01 — Administrator can access schedule publication option (Main Success Path)
**Priority:** P0  
**Preconditions:** Administrator authenticated; finalized schedule exists.  
**Steps:**
1. Log in as administrator (ADM-01).
2. Navigate to the conference schedule management area.
3. Select a finalized schedule.
**Expected Results:**
- CMS displays the selected schedule.
- “Publish Schedule” option is visible and enabled.

---

## UC-25-AT-02 — Successful publication of finalized schedule (Main Success Path)
**Priority:** P0  
**Preconditions:** Finalized schedule selected.  
**Steps:**
1. Select “Publish Schedule.”
2. Confirm the publication action.
**Expected Results:**
- CMS marks the schedule as the final published version.
- CMS stores publication status in the database.
- CMS displays confirmation of successful publication.

---

## UC-25-AT-03 — Published schedule is publicly accessible (Main Success Path)
**Priority:** P0  
**Preconditions:** Schedule successfully published.  
**Steps:**
1. Log out of the CMS.
2. Access the CMS website as a guest user.
3. Navigate to the conference schedule section.
**Expected Results:**
- Published schedule is visible and accessible without authentication.
- Schedule details (sessions, times, locations) display correctly.

---

## UC-25-AT-04 — Notifications sent to authors and attendees (Main Success Path)
**Priority:** P0  
**Preconditions:** Schedule successfully published; notification service operational.  
**Steps:**
1. Publish the finalized schedule.
2. Check email inboxes for registered authors and attendees.
**Expected Results:**
- Authors and attendees receive notification indicating schedule publication.
- Notification contains a valid link or instructions to view the schedule.

---

## UC-25-AT-05 — Notification delivery failure handling (Extension 7a)
**Priority:** P1  
**Preconditions:** Simulate notification service outage.  
**Steps:**
1. Publish the finalized schedule.
**Expected Results:**
- CMS logs notification delivery failure.
- Schedule is still published and publicly accessible.
- Administrator receives confirmation of publication with warning about notifications.

---

## UC-25-AT-06 — Database unavailable during publication (Extension 6a)
**Priority:** P0  
**Preconditions:** Simulate CMS database outage.  
**Steps:**
1. Attempt to publish the finalized schedule.
**Expected Results:**
- CMS displays an error indicating the schedule could not be published.
- Schedule publication status remains unchanged.
- No notifications are sent.

---

## UC-25-AT-07 — Administrator cancels publication process (Extension 4a)
**Priority:** P1  
**Preconditions:** Administrator has initiated publication.  
**Steps:**
1. Select “Publish Schedule.”
2. Cancel the publication confirmation.
**Expected Results:**
- CMS aborts publication.
- Schedule remains unpublished.
- No notifications are sent.

---

## UC-25-AT-08 — Attempt to publish a non-finalized schedule (Extension 3a)
**Priority:** P0  
**Preconditions:** Schedule exists but is not finalized.  
**Steps:**
1. Select a non-finalized schedule.
2. Attempt to publish it.
**Expected Results:**
- CMS blocks publication.
- CMS displays a message indicating the schedule must be finalized first.
- No publication or notifications occur.

---

## UC-25-AT-09 — Web server unavailable during publication (Extension 1a)
**Priority:** P0  
**Preconditions:** Simulate CMS web server outage.  
**Steps:**
1. Attempt to access the publication feature.
**Expected Results:**
- CMS displays a system error page.
- No changes are made to schedule status.
- No notifications are sent.

---

## UC-25-AT-10 — Published schedule persistence
**Priority:** P1  
**Preconditions:** Schedule successfully published.  
**Steps:**
1. Log out and log back in as administrator.
2. Reopen the published schedule.
**Expected Results:**
- Schedule remains marked as published.
- Publication timestamp and status are retained.
