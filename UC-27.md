# Use Case UC-27: Pay Conference Registration Fee

**Goal in Context**:  
Allow an attendee to pay the conference registration fee online so that their attendance is confirmed.

**Scope**:  
Conference Management System (CMS)

**Level**:  
User Goal

**Primary Actor**:  
Attendee

**Secondary Actors**:  
CMS Web Interface  
CMS Database  
Payment System (external)

**Trigger**:  
The attendee submits payment information for conference registration.

---

## Success End Condition
* The registration fee is successfully paid, and the attendee’s participation is confirmed.

## Failed End Condition
* The payment is not completed, and the attendee’s registration remains unconfirmed.

---

## Preconditions
* The attendee is registered and authenticated in the CMS.
* The attendee has initiated conference registration.
* Conference pricing information is available.
* The online payment system is available.

---

## Main Success Scenario
1. The attendee logs into the CMS.
2. The attendee navigates to the conference registration payment page.
3. The CMS displays the payment interface with registration fee details.
4. The attendee enters valid payment information.
5. The attendee submits the payment.
6. The CMS sends the payment details to the external payment system.
7. The payment system processes the transaction successfully.
8. The CMS records the payment confirmation in the database.
9. The CMS marks the attendee’s registration as confirmed.
10. The CMS sends a payment confirmation notification to the attendee.

---

## Extensions

* **7a**: Payment is declined by the payment system  
    * **7a1**: The CMS displays a payment failure message and allows the attendee to retry or cancel.

* **6a**: Payment system is unavailable  
    * **6a1**: The CMS displays an error message indicating that payment cannot be processed at this time.

* **4a**: Attendee enters invalid or incomplete payment information  
    * **4a1**: The CMS displays validation errors and prompts the attendee to correct the information.

* **5a**: Attendee cancels payment  
    * **5a1**: The CMS aborts the payment process and leaves the registration unconfirmed.

* **8a**: CMS database is unavailable  
    * **8a1**: The CMS displays an error message indicating that payment confirmation could not be recorded.

* **1a**: CMS web server is unavailable  
    * **1a1**: The CMS displays a system error page indicating temporary service unavailability.

---

## Related Information
* **Priority**: High  
* **Frequency**: Once per attendee  
* **Open Issues**:  
  * Whether refunds or partial payments are supported  
  * Whether payment receipts should be downloadable
